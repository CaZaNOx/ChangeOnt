# Gödel is a methodological diagnostic, not a metaphysical guarantor

AI_13 warns that “Gödel-driven dynamics” do not follow from the immediate datum. Gödel’s theorems ensure limits of formal systems, but they do not decree how physical or experiential change must proceed. Treat Gödel and “hole” language as methodological diagnostics—signals that a description class cannot close residuals—rather than as proofs that reality has specific gaps. If you want “no minimal step,” “no halt,” or guaranteed emergence ladders, promote those to explicit CO axioms (CO1–CO4) and own the commitments. Once the axioms are stated, Gödel pressure, attractors, and emergence become concrete tools: ways to log which regions resist closure and to motivate new variables, not metaphysical claims smuggled in from incompleteness.
