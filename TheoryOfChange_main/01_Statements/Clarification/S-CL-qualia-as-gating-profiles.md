# VO reinterprets qualia as gating profiles over importance channels

In the German section (lines 18364‑18391) VO defines intensity as the current strength of the Wichtigkeits-Gate; “rot”, “Schmerz”, etc., are rule profiles describing which channel gets how much gain and when. Measuring qualia means operationalizing latency, precision, self-write, and gate dynamics physiologically and behaviorally; the process becomes Vorhersage → Fehler + Präzision → Wichtigkeits-Gate → Self-Write → Update. This reframes qualia as structured control profiles rather than ontologically separate entities.
