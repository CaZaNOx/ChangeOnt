# Constructive criticism increases SRL and SE
Corollary: When criticism is tied to explicit transformations (refined criteria, clarified invariants, or abstention gates), structural recurrence likelihood (SRL) improves and stabilization energy (SE) increases for valid identities under the evaluation surface.

Philosophical translation
- Productive critique sharpens recurring structure and makes identity loops harder to break.
