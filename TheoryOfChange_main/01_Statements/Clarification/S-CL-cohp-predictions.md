# CO-HP yields concrete predictions linking invariants to data

AI_13 enumerates falsifiable predictions: (1) presence \(P\) tracks level of consciousness—drops to ~0 under deep anesthesia, persists internally in REM, distinguishes locked-in vs unconscious; (2) valence equals urgency curvature—manipulating neuromodulators that implement urgency changes \(V\) and reported affect together; (3) qualia coordinates \(Q\) align with action-stabilized perceptual constancy, so perturbations (e.g., illumination shifts) follow the invariant geometry; (4) salience gradients map to attentional MEG/EEG signatures and reaction times; (5) cross-modal transport of \(\Phi\) predicts report/behavior in new contexts. These predictions let the theory be broken.
