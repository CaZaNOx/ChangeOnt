# Define a change operator that preserves overlap while introducing new difference

Characterize change as an operator `PropertyChange(X_n)` that produces the next difference-pattern `X_{n+1}` such that `0 < Similarity(X_n, X_{n+1}) < 1`. Similarity is computed purely over the relational differences `R(X)`; the operator must retain at least one existing relation while adding at least one novel one. If total identity or total disjunction occurs, the operator is forced to break stasis by restoring partial overlap. Mapping to automata/categorical analogies ensures no substrate is reintroduced.
