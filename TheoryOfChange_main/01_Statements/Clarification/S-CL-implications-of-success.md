# Success yields a physics of experience and machine benchmarks

If the program holds, you obtain (AI_13 lines 17410‑17460): (1) a practical “physics of experience” that predicts how to move agents into desired experiential states (therapy, anesthesia, UX) with quantified uncertainty; (2) a benchmark for machine systems—any AI claiming consciousness must satisfy the invariant suite, transport tests, and gauge constraints, not just output fluent text; (3) actionable design levers for valence, urgency, and dwell. These implications motivate the heavy validation protocols outlined earlier.
