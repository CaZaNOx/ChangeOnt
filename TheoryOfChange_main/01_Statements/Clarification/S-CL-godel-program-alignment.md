# Use Gödel pressure plus attractors as the program lens, but pin the primitives

AI_13’s program review (lines 23803‑23902) recommends centering the research narrative on two coupled primitives: Gödel pressure/holes mark where limits trigger phase shifts, and attractors/urgency explain how subjects re-pattern to cross those limits. To keep that lens scientific, fix the vocabulary (T4 subjects-as-patterns, T5 consciousness-as-urgency), define the mathematical substrate (state space, topology/metric, update map), and publish falsifiability hooks (what data would contradict continuity claims, urgency-as-update, or alignment protocols). Scope creep—applying Gödel talk to cosmology, computation, and consciousness simultaneously—dilutes traction; instead, deliver a minimal “Becoming → Prediction” note with one reproducible figure, then scale out. Precise primitives + Gödel/attractor diagnostics give Change Ontology a distinctive, testable identity.
