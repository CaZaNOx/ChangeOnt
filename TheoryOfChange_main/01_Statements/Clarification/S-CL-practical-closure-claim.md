# Hard problem reduces to indexical gauge once no predictive remainder remains

AI_13 clarifies that even after perfect transport and invariant matching you have a coverage proof, not a logical identity. When every intervention’s outcome is captured (transport, lesions, valence flips), “redness” claims become transportable and testably equivalent up to gauge; no empirical remainder remains. The metaphysical zombie objection devolves into insisting on extra facts beyond gauge choice. This is the honest boundary: science achieves practical closure by eliminating all actionable gaps, not by proving metaphysical necessity. Equivalently (lines 17987‑17995), “what-it’s-like” is nothing over and above the gauge-fixed invariants you can measure, predict, and control; the loop’s lawful pattern replaces the ghost.
