# Seed foundational notes that formalize Gödel-hole limits and experiments

AI_13 prescribes the next build steps: extend the foundational series with _FND_9 Detectability Thresholds_ and _FND_10 Gödel-Holes_ capturing how finite observers create principled blind spots; mirror those in 01_CoreOntology so every formal primitive (Immediate Datum, Tx operator, Gödel incompleteness) has a canonical home; and prove at least one bound—a no-free-lunch or undecidability-style theorem—that translates “Gödel hole” into a finite-observer limit. Without that scaffolding, talk of Gödel-hole density or Tx vagueness devolves into poetry. With it, experiments and audits gain crisp targets (which questions become unanswerable at which resolutions, how detectors fail, etc.).
