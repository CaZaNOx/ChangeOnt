# Core CO axiom (CO-0) forbids global stasis without forcing continuity

## Statement (clarification)
CO‑0: change is happening now and never globally stops (no open interval of total stasis). CO‑0 alone gives scale‑free change; continuity still requires an added bounded‑rate/No‑Leap premise. This clarification separates the ontological commitment from extra modeling and empirical hypotheses.

## Philosophical Justification
- From [[S-FT-immediate-datum]], change/experience is given; asserting an interval of total stasis is performatively unstable.
- Scale‑free change follows if no minimum Δ is assumed, but differentiability is not forced; explicit No‑Leap is needed to exclude jumps.
- Distinguishing ontology vs. modeling avoids overstating what CO‑0 alone entails.

## Clarifications / Further Context
- Ontology: change-first flow; Gödel pressure marks residual gaps.
- Modeling stances: metastable patterns; predictive-control framing of consciousness.
- Empirical hooks: operator drift ↔ resilience; subjective intensity ∝ update gradients; relational observability; latent variation budgets; consciousness assay suggestions.

## Next Steps in Chain
- suggest: formalize/accept/reject No‑Leap or bounded‑rate premises.
- suggest: audit consciousness/assay proposals and alignment pilots separately as empirical programs.
