# Project Profile — Vision, Audience, Usage, Style

Vision
- Provide a rigorous philosophical foundation for the ontology of change with practical bridges to cognition and AI.

Intended Audience
- Professional philosophers, cognitive scientists, AI researchers, and advanced readers seeking foundational clarity.

Usage and Impact
- Reframes parts of metaphysics, epistemology, mind, and cognitive science; aims to inform future AI/human systems.

Style & Tone
- Rigorous and precise; measured and humble; formal but accessible.
- Definitions provided on first use; internal links for immediate clarity; consistent terminology.

Rules for AI Assistance (editorial)
- Do not introduce speculative or ungrounded claims.
- Respect defined terminology; provide clear rationale for edits.
- Prefer clarity, structure, and reader guidance.
