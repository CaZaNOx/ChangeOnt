# Subject as projection plus attention on the global flow

AI_13 gives the crisp monist account: there is only the global flow \(C\) with generator \(F_{\theta_t}\) and meta-flow \(H\). A subject \(S = (\pi_S, a_S)\) is a cut (projection + attention weighting) that selects a region of the state space. Pushforward of the world density \(\rho_t\) through the subject’s projection and attention produces the present field \(\mu_t^{(S)}\); spikes in the induced flow \(\Phi^{(S)}\) correspond to experienced salience. This keeps ontology single (no dual substances) while explaining how different “faces” (world vs subject) arise as structured projections with shared invariants.
