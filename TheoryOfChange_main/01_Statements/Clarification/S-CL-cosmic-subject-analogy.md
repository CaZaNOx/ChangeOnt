# Cosmic horizons mirror subject gates, traces, and value loops

AI_13 draws the cosmology analogy: the universe as a self-maintaining loop inherits the same rungs as subjects—boundary (causal horizon), value/urgency (principles weighting histories), attention/gate (state-dependent exchange via horizons), trace (symmetry-breaking fossils), and model (coarse-grained laws). Horizon shielding limits destabilizing coordination just like attention gates; conserved charges/topological sectors act as guard rails akin to subject traces; universality explains why few macroscopic knobs stabilize dynamics. This frames cosmology as a macro-level subject loop without committing to a specific micro-ontology.
