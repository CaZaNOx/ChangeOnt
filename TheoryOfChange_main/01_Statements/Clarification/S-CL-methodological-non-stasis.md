# Methodological Non-Stasis (MNS) treats global freeze as illegible, not impossible

AI_13 clarifies the FND_0 stance: “change never stops” is a methodological commitment (MNS), not a logical theorem. MNS says: from the immediate datum we refuse hypotheses that posit an absolute stasis followed by a restart, because such events cannot be made intelligible within the DS frame. Observation & coarsening explains apparent discreteness as thresholding, and Identity-through-change defines subjects as processes whose compression operator \(C\) keeps invariants under small bends. Continuity remains optional; you may add a tol/scale postulate later, but MNS itself only bans ungrounded global freezes. Writing this as an explicit postulate keeps the document honest and separates methodological hygiene from modal claims.
