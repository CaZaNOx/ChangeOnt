# Gödel hole review loop
## Claim (formal)
At each breath cycle, review GH registry; attempt resolution via Tx or refactor; if unresolved, reschedule and update context. Convergence implies closure; divergence implies model extension or re‑scoping.

## Philosophical Translation (of formal claim)
Gaps are not static; we revisit them as the theory breathes and gains structure.
