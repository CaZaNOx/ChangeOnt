# Pointer behavior under frame shifts (Tx)
## Claim (formal)
Under Tx: (S,F)→(S',F'), pointers update via meta‑pointer ⇘² to maintain structural reference: Pointer'(Now') = Update_Tx(Pointer(Now), Tx), preserving reach relations up to frame transformation.

## Philosophical Translation (of formal claim)
When the frame changes, pointers shouldn’t break — they should be carried forward in a principled way, tracked by meta‑pointers.

## Clarifications / Further Context
- ⇘² audits pointer stability; collapse ⊘ marks failures to transport pointers across frames.
