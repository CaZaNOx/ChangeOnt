# Ethical humility — convergence to adequacy, not absolute completion
## Statement (corollary)
Shared gauges stabilize toward adequate coherence; claims to final, total objectivity are structurally misguided under self‑containment limits.

## Philosophical Translation
Truth together is good‑enough alignment, not God’s‑eye completion.
