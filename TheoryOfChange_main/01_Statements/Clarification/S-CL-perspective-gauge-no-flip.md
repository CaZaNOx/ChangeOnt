# Perspective gauge and no-flip constraint lock phenomenology to dynamics

To make “experience = Φ-invariants” precise, AI_13 defines a perspective gauge GF* (unique diffeomorphism minimizing counterfactual prediction error subject to body/clock anchoring and continuity). Once GF* is fixed, the no-flip constraint states that any transformation altering Φ must degrade at least one control constraint (constancy, cross-modal alignment, outcome statistics, reports). Together they yield the CO-Identity theorem: two agents share experience iff their Φ match (up to smooth reparameterization); zombies and inverted qualia are impossible without detectable costs.
