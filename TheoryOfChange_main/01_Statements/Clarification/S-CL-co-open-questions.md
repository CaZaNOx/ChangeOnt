# Change Ontology open questions span continuity, identity, Gödel holes, and manifolds

AI_13 lists eight research drivers: (1) No-minimal-delta lemmas (prove density in change rather than assume it); (2) Identity-through-change operators/invariants; (3) Gödel-hole formalization (computability vs structural instability); (4) Layer criteria (when do new coarse-grain coordinates emerge?); (5) Subject-specific manifolds with valence gradients; (6) Computability class of CO (GPAC vs Turing vs super-Turing); (7) Natural geometry (Riemannian, Finsler, information-geometric); (8) Emotion/urgency experiments (manipulate stakes, map physiology + reports). The note ensures these targets are tracked explicitly so the program has clear math and experimental to-dos beyond rhetoric.
