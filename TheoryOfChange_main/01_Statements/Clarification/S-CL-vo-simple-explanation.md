# Change ontology’s lay explanation of the hard problem (German summary)

Following the classical primer, AI_13 offers a German lay summary of how Veränderungsontologie approaches consciousness: experience isn’t a mysterious extra, but the stable, importance-weighted pattern of a changing system viewed from one perspective. Gödel-löcher stand for structural gaps that force new layers/invariants. The explanation emphasizes that VO doesn’t provide a purely logical proof, but an identification thesis backed by empirical tests: qualia become the profiles of channels (which gate how strongly, when, and for whom). This satisfies onboarding needs for readers who want a non-technical description of why VO claims to resolve the gap.
