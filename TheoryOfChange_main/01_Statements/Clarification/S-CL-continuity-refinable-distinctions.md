# Continuity arises from unceasing change plus refinable distinctions

AI_13 clarifies the logical path: to avoid importing classical assumptions, Change Ontology should state two explicit postulates—(H) unceasing change along our causal line (no total stop) and (R) refinability of distinctions (any detectable transition can be subdivided by refining the subject’s cut). With (H) and (R), discreteness becomes local (detection thresholds), continuity is the ability to refine the cut, and gaps would contradict the axioms. Without them, continuity does not follow. The advice: say plainly “discreteness is a property of the current cut; continuity is the ability to refine it,” and avoid overclaiming about the universe’s substrate. This makes the “no minimal step” argument precise and auditable.
