# Tier every claim (logical, model, empirical, speculative) and pre-register falsifiers

AI_13 insists that Change Ontology statements be tagged by evidential tier: LOGICAL (what follows from CO-0/CO-1), MODELING STANCE (metastable patterns, Gödel-pressure metaphors), EMPIRICAL PROGRAM (cross-subject transformations, affect-as-update assays), or SPECULATIVE extrapolation (spiral life-cycles, gravity analogies). Each non-logical claim must name falsifiers (e.g., urgency manipulations failing to shift reports/attention/physiology; hole-density dashboards that do not forecast innovation zones) and cite the measurements that would contradict it. This discipline protects the project from metaphor drift (“Gödel proves metaphysics”) and keeps ambitious integration work auditable while still highlighting signature deliverables: measurable Gödel-pressure, attractor-based identity, cross-domain translation protocols, early-warning dashboards for regime change, and the 30-second pitch (“identity is metastable pattern; laws are regularities of change; novelty shows up where Gödel-pressure spikes”).
