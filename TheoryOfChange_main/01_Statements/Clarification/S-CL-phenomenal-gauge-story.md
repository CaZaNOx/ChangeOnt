# Treat residual “this-ness” as gauge once invariants commute

AI_13 argues that the final “mystery” must be reframed as gauge freedom (lines 17170‑17218). Each subject carries a private coordinate chart \(S_i\); the admissible reparameterizations form a gauge group \(G_i\). To finish the bridge, one must: (1) specify \(G\) (allowed monotone rescalings, affect/attention warps) and justify it empirically; (2) identify gauge-invariant quantities in the canonical latent \(Z^*\); (3) prove all testable claims reduce to those invariants; and (4) show that consciousness equals realizing \(\mathcal{J}\) up to \(G\), while any leftover “this-ness” is just a coordinate choice. Cross-subject transport, holonomy-style integrals, and commuting counterfactuals quantify the gauge residue; when it vanishes, no extra metaphysical fact remains.
