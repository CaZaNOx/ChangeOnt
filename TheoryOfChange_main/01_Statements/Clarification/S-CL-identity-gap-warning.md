# Recognize the residual identity gap even with intersubject transport

Even after gauge-limited intersubject transport works, AI_13 cautions (lines 17429‑17448) that the operational gap differs from the metaphysical one: the framework lets you transport experiential states up to measured residue, but it still doesn’t logically explain why this structure _is_ redness rather than merely correlating with it. The honest stance is to declare operational victory (no predictive remainder) while being explicit that metaphysical identity is asserted, not derived; overclaiming invites failure.
