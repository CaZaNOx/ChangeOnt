# Phenomenal Gödel holes are persistent pockets of surprise in a subject’s loop

AI_13 interprets Gödel holes at the subjective level: they are pockets of surprise that persist despite attention and practice, signaling limits of the current model. If repeated refinement cannot span two anchors with any low-cost path, you’ve located a Gödel-pressure region that predicts innovation or failure. This matches the phenomenology (felt mismatch, urgency spikes) and links to subject-level diagnostics (loop re-entry, mismatch curves).
