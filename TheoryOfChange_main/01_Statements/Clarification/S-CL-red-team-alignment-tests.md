# Run adversarial and edge-case tests against the invariant suite

To guard against false positives, AI_13 recommends a standing red-team: (1) train large RL agents with memory/affect-mimic objectives to see whether they can satisfy the invariants without the self-model \(J_2\); (2) map anesthesia induction/recovery to confirm geometry collapse and rebound; (3) test identical twins vs strangers to see whether gauge residue scales with biological similarity; (4) cross cultures to confirm affect fields align (rank order preserved after monotone warp). Passing these adversarial checks demonstrates that the invariant suite is non-trivial and genuinely tied to conscious structure.
