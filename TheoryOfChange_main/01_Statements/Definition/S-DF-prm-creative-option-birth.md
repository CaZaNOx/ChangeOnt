# Primitive — Creative option birth (holes/novelty with bounded depth)
## Claim (formal)
Propose actions from Gödel‑hole loci or novelty peaks, constrained by breath‑phase and depth limits to avoid destabilization.

## Philosophical Translation (of formal claim)
Make new moves where you know you don’t know — but don’t blow yourself up.

## Philosophical Justification
- [[S-DF-godel-hole-pointer]] marks gaps; exploring from GH targets true unknowns.
- [[S-DF-breath-field-global-integrator]] and depth/breadth limits keep exploration within SE budgets and coherence.
- Bounded exploration prevents collapse while still harvesting novelty.

## Clarifications / Further Context
- Feeds EH/EJ policy and EB/EF routers.
- Depth bounds and breath phase should be declared; log options generated and their impact on GH/SE.

## Next Steps in Chain
- integrate with routers and audit to track outcomes of creative births.
