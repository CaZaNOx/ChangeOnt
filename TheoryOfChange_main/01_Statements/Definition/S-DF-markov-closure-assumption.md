# Markov closure — fixed state space and closed transitions
## Claim (formal)
Classical Markovian modeling assumes a fixed state space S and transitions T: S→S with closure (no change to S).

## Philosophical Translation (of formal claim)
The model’s world is fixed; only positions within it change.

## Clarifications / Further Context
- Hidden Markov models still fix parameterization of S.
