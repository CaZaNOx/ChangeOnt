# Hard vs soft consciousness problems explained for lay readers (German summary)

AI_13 provides a plain-language German primer: “weiche” Probleme (attention, perception, memory, decision, reportability) are functional questions addressed by normal science (input–processing–output). The “harte Problem” asks why any of those processes feel like something from the inside—why qualia exist at all. The text covers why it is considered “hard” (perspective gap, missing explanatory form, lack of decisive tests) and sketches classical response families (physicalism, dualism, panpsychism, illusionism) plus what would count as progress (bridge laws with predictive bite, coverage of new cases, necessity not mere correlation). This fills the onboarding need for readers who know only the classical framing.
