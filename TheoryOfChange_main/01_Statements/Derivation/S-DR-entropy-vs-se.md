# Entropy vs Stabilization Energy
## Claim (formal)
High Σ with low SE indicates fragile diversity (novelty/instability); high Σ with high SE indicates robust creativity; low Σ with high SE indicates rigid stability.

## Philosophical Translation (of formal claim)
How many different ways back matters, but so does how reliably they hold.
