# Reactivation of stored tension enables cross-experience integration
## Statement (corollary)
Reactivating a stored tension (difference) lets a subject compare, align, and integrate later experiences with earlier distinctions.

## Philosophical Translation
Keeping a live thread of difference lets you stitch moments together.
