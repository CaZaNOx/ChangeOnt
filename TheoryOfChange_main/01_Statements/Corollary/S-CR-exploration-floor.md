# Exploration floor — non-zero exploration follows from incompleteness
## Statement (corollary)
Agents require a non‑zero exploration temperature/breadth to remain adequate under Gödel limits; otherwise they risk lock‑in and unmodeled failures.

## Philosophical Translation
Keep trying new things a little bit, always.
