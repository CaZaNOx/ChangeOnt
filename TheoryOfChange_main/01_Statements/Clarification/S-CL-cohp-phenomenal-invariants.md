# CO-HP defines consciousness via gauge-fixed PAL invariants

The CO-HP proposal anchors the closed perception–action–learning loop via a perspective gauge (actual Now/Here/Me) so automorphisms that would permute qualia no longer apply. Phenomenal character becomes the tuple \(\Phi = (P,S,V,Q,\ldots)\): presence \(P\) (closed-loop gain), salience \(S\) (attention-weighted utility gradient), valence \(V\) (signed curvature of predicted error-to-go under urgency), and low-dimensional qualia coordinates \(Q\) defined by their role in action-stable constancy. The claim is that a state is conscious iff the self-field \(I\) is engaged and \(\Phi\) is non-degenerate; “what-it’s-like” just is \(\Phi\) at the gauge-fixed point.
