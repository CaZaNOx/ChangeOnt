# Markov Closure Limits
Overview:
Classical Markovian modeling assumes a fixed state space and closure under transition. Change ontology introduces frame‑level change (Tx) and structure‑shifting dynamics, violating fixed‑space closure. Gödel holes mark the structural boundary where current representation cannot encode the needed linkage.
