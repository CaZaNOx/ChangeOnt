# Raise classical IQ proxies and change-intellect by closing specific gaps

## Statement (clarification)
Two upgrade tracks: (1) Classical IQ proxies: focus on speed/precision, short-horizon working memory, fast visuo-spatial compression via short daily sprints (timed matrices, digit-symbol/search, mental arithmetic ladders, n-back) plus sleep/cardio hygiene. (2) Change-intellect (CII): deliverables are crisp primitives, a minimal formal core, toy worlds with CO-predictive edge, measurable mappings for philosophical terms, and explicit falsifiers.

## Philosophical Justification
- Classical metrics are rate/bandwidth limited; targeted drills raise ceiling without changing ontology.
- CII is measured by operationalizing CO: primitives + formal rules + predictive demos + measurable mappings + falsifiers turn rhetoric into auditable practice, aligning with change-fitness.

## Clarifications / Further Context
- This is a practice/gap-closure guide, not a theorem.
- Toy worlds and falsifiers should link to audit/benchmark protocols to avoid hand-waving.
- Keep psychometric drills separate from CO-theory claims; they are supportive, not evidential.

## Next Steps in Chain
- suggest: log CII deliverables in audit trail and tie to benchmark protocols.
- suggest: document toy-world experiments and their predicted differentials vs classical frames.
