# Align physical and phenomenal spaces via gauge-bounded transforms

To claim closure you must show a structure-preserving transform between the information/physical space \(S\) (broadcast, self-model dynamics) and the phenomenal space \(P\) (similarity/contrast geometry). AI_13 lines 16929‑17016 demand the mapping satisfy: topological preservation (no tearing), compositionality (part-whole relations), bounded distortion (bi-Lipschitz / isometry on key subspaces), uniqueness up to gauge, and necessity (ablating self-inclusion, valence, or dwell collapses the mapped phenomenal axes).
