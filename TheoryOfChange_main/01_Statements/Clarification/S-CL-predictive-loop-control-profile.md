# Change ontology defines consciousness as the control profile of predictive loops

AI_13 states explicitly (lines 18404‑18419) that two systems with near-identical \(\langle e,\pi,G,\tau,\omega\rangle\) trajectories (error, precision, gating, latency, inertia) over an interval share near-identical phenomenology; the “gap” collapses to measuring and manipulating these fields. Conscious experience is the real-time control profile of a predictive loop. Experimental falsifiers include selective ablations of \(\tau\), \(\pi\), \(G\), or \(\omega\) via pharmacology or tasks to see whether predicted phenomenological changes occur.
