# Model non-stationary affect fields and kernels explicitly

AI_13 stresses that the affect field \(A(x)\) and kernels \(K_u\) drift with learning, fatigue, circadian phase, and state transitions (sleep, anesthesia, psychedelics). Treating them as stationary causes alignment failures. The fix is to model controlled SDEs or time-varying parameters, include time/context as explicit coordinates, and stress-test across transitions (sleep onset/offset, anesthesia emergence, micro-dosed psychedelics) plus population controls (identical twins vs strangers, cross-cultural affect ranks). Consciousness invariants must remain lawful through these drifts; otherwise the canonical latent is not real.
