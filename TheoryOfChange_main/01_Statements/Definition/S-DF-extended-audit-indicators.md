# Extended audit indicators — operational hooks
## Claim (formal)
Implement detection hooks for indicators: variable birth/death logs, boundary deformation metrics, nonlocal link rise, new attractor alerts, and Tx proposals with GH cross‑links.

## Philosophical Translation (of formal claim)
We don’t just define indicators — we measure them in running systems.

## Philosophical Justification
- [[S-DF-nonclassical-indicators]] lists structural signals; without instrumentation they remain theoretical.
- [[S-DF-cross-audit-markov-gh-tx]] prescribes what to do when closure fails; hooking indicators to audits makes the response automatic and accountable.

## Clarifications / Further Context
- Ties audit output to GH registry and Tx specifications for accountability.
- Hooks should log thresholds, detections, and actions taken for later review.

## Next Steps in Chain
- suggest: integrate with change-benchmark protocol for standardized reporting.
