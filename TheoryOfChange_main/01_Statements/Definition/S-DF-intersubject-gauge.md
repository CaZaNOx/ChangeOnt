# Inter-subject gauge / shared reality field
## Claim (formal)
A shared alignment field calibrates representations across subjects, enabling consistency of identity/invariants and validation exchanges.

## Philosophical Translation (of formal claim)
We coordinate our worlds by aligning how we measure and stabilize patterns.

## Philosophical Justification
Given agents with their own gauges and evolving self-models, cross-agent validation requires aligning measurement/representation so invariants and RTV results are comparable. The intersubject gauge supplies that shared alignment field; without it, exchanges misfire and identity/invariant claims fragment.
