# No privileged substrate/hidden reality (QM framing)
## Claim (formal)
Under Tx framing, appeals to a fixed hidden substrate to restore closure are unjustified; model Tx explicitly or log GH where needed.

## Philosophical Translation (of formal claim)
Don’t smuggle back a fixed world behind the one that changes; treat frame change as first‑class.
