# Formalize Gödel holes via undecidability, noncomputability, or structural instabilities

AI_13 lists the research questions that turn “Gödel hole” from metaphor into math: specify whether a hole is undecidability within a model class, a noncomputable subset of the state space, or a structural instability (sensitivity blow-up) in a flow; determine where holes appear (metastability breakdown, under-determination, cross-layer gaps); and tie each option to measurable surrogates. This commits the program to computability-theoretic and dynamical-systems criteria rather than vague appeals to incompleteness.
